Company Overview

Nike, Inc., named after the Greek winged goddess of victory, is the world's leading athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment manufacturer.

Our Mission

To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world. And if you have a body, you are an athlete.

Our Values

  • Inspiration: We strive to inspire greatness in all that we do.
  • Innovation: Pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
  • Sustainability: Committed to creating a more sustainable future.

Our History

Founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight as Blue Ribbon Sports, the company officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971.
